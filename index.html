SongSync

I regret to inform you that SongSync has been canceled, and is no longer in development.

From the start, I had high hopes for SongSync. I started work on it in August of 2019 as a simple Glitch app, and it quickly started to grow in both size and amount of effort required.

As the semester progressed and new projects came about, I found myself working on SongSync less and less frequently. Now, with a new semester underway, my other app demanding more attention, and now COVID-19 putting my state in lockdown, I realized I have too much on my plate.

Furthermore, this project was way too ambitious for me to take on myself. I didn't want to force my way through and deliver a subpar experience.

I recently realized that I hadn't worked on SongSync on months. Instead of leaving the project in limbo, I wanted to give it a proper sendoff.

Thank you to everyone who believed in me and in the project. I hope you understand my decision.

- Justin
